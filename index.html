About Artist

Jhene Aiko is an American R&B singer songwriter. She started her career in 2006 by contributing vocals to B2k. By the age of 15 years old, she had an album ready to be released but she decided to take a pause on her career and focus on school work. Although, in 2011 she came back a released her mixtape 'Sailing Souls" on her own website.She later then signs with record producer No I.D.
